HOME / ABOUT US

We are proud to be disruptive consultants.

Emerging Tech was founded with the aim of fulfilling the emergent need for standards-based, end-to-end IT consultancy. Our experts are located across the United States, eager to design and implement cutting-edge solutions that revolutionize our partners' capabilities.

Who we are

Standards-based. End to end.

Our range of experience crosses multiple industries and sectors, with deep knowledge of mobile health care, cybersecurity, CSfC, systems engineering, network engineering, Unified Endpoint Management (UEM), and Mobile Application Management (MAM).

Our Centers of Excellence are dedicated to researching, developing, and deploying innovative solutions designed to fulfill client needs — incorporating comprehensive, pragmatic evaluations designed to improve outcomes and enhance customer experience.
