Healthcare Modernization — connected care at scale.

Transforming federal health IT through EHR integration, clinical workflow optimization, and interoperable care platforms — aligning technology, regulation, and clinical realities.

Strategy through deployment and sustainment

Our approach

Emerging Tech supports enterprise EHR modernization from strategy through deployment and sustainment. We focus on integrating legacy systems, improving interoperability, and enabling scalable platforms that support real-world care delivery.

Our teams partner with federal health agencies to modernize legacy clinical systems, integrate EHR platforms, and improve care coordination — while maintaining compliance with security and privacy requirements. Beyond EHR platforms, we deliver the integration layer that connects clinical devices, administrative systems, and data analytics, creating a unified care environment.

We bring deep hands-on experience with the platforms that power federal healthcare — from legacy VistA environments to modern Oracle Health (Cerner) and Epic deployments, with health data interoperability built on HL7 FHIR.

What we deliver
  • EHR integration across clinical and administrative systems
  • Improve care coordination through optimized workflows
  • Integrate clinical, financial, and analytics systems
  • Align platforms with security, privacy, and interoperability requirements
  • Support ATO, NIST RMF, Zero Trust, and ongoing cybersecurity operations
