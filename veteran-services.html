Veterans Services in service of those who served.

Emerging Tech supports Veterans by delivering impactful solutions that improve access to care, benefits, and services — enterprise-scale VA healthcare IT, EHR modernization, cybersecurity, and cloud from a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business.

The mission environment

How we serve

Federal veteran programs operate within one of the most complex and highly regulated environments in government. The VA must deliver secure, uninterrupted healthcare and benefits services to millions of veterans across distributed clinical, administrative, and digital systems — environments that demand continuous availability, strong cybersecurity controls, and interoperability across legacy and modern platforms.

Emerging Tech supports large-scale VA modernization initiatives by designing, securing, integrating, and sustaining mission systems that operate at enterprise scale. We understand the operational realities of clinical workflows, benefits processing, cybersecurity governance, and cross-agency coordination — and we translate modernization objectives into deployable, compliant, and resilient solutions.

Mission-first architecture designed around veteran care, security embedded by design across the lifecycle, and pragmatic execution within high-visibility, regulated federal environments.
