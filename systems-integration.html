Technology & System Integration — one coherent enterprise.

Enterprise systems integration, data engineering, cloud and DevSecOps modernization, and digital transformation — enabling interoperability and a common standard across diverse IT environments.

Documented, validated, sustained

Our approach

Emerging Tech reduces the likelihood of failure through extensive documentation and recording before, during, and after solution design and implementation. We utilize Independent Verification and Validation (IV&V) to perform unbiased assessments, ensuring each system is built in accordance with its design specifications.

We specialize in mobile technology — iOS, Android, and Windows-based devices — and our Mobile Innovation Lab (MIL) provides on-demand services anywhere, anytime. Our 123-security strategy covers (1) mobile device platform security, (2) mobile application protection, and (3) mobile data protection at rest and in transit, with military-grade configurations protecting sensitive data.

Our lifecycle runs assess → develop → implement → deploy → sustain: we assess existing infrastructure for abnormalities, develop cost-effective solutions, migrate into production with additional testing, and support every product through and beyond its initial launch.

What we deliver
  • Enterprise systems integration and interoperability
  • Mobile technology across iOS, Android, and Windows devices
  • Independent Verification and Validation (IV&V) at every phase
  • Unified Endpoint Management (UEM) and Mobile Application Management (MAM)
  • Data engineering and DevSecOps modernization
